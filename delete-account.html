Delete your IoTFlow account and data

Last updated: 17 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your IoTFlow account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

  1. Email angch@tertiaryinfotech.com from the email address registered to your IoTFlow account, with the subject "IoTFlow account deletion request".
  2. We will verify your identity and delete your account within 30 days.
  3. If you run a self-hosted IoTFlow server, your server administrator can delete your account and data directly from the server database at any time.

What data is deleted

Upon a verified request we delete your account record (name, email, password hash) and the devices, device tokens, telemetry and alerts associated with your account. This data is permanently removed.

What may be kept

We may retain minimal records required by law or for security/fraud-prevention for up to 90 days, after which they are deleted.
