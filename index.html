IoTFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

IoTFlow ("the app") is the mobile client for the IoTFlow IoT platform, published by Tertiary Infotech. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Information we collect

  • Account information — when you sign in or register, your name, email address and password are sent to the IoTFlow server you connect to (by default iot.tertiaryinfotech.com, or any self-hosted instance you configure) to authenticate your session.
  • Device & telemetry data — information about the IoT devices you register (names, types, protocols, locations, telemetry readings and alerts) that you choose to manage through the app.

How we use it

Data is used solely to authenticate you and to display your devices, telemetry and alerts. The app communicates only with the IoTFlow server you configure, over HTTPS.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. The app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics SDKs. Your data resides on the IoTFlow server you connect to, under your or your organisation's control.

Data retention & deletion

Account and device data is stored on your IoTFlow server. You can delete devices in the app, and you may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us or through your server administrator.

Security

All network communication uses HTTPS. Session credentials are stored only on your device.

Children

IoTFlow is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13.
